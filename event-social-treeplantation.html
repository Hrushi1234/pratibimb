Tree Plantation

Tree Plantation : The Pratibimb Committee is always striving to contribute to the society one way or another. All the committee members of pratibimb as well as the students of VJTI planted trees such as gulmohar, mango,neem and curry in the surrounding areas. Thus , Pratibimb is trying to maintain an environmental balance in the society.
