Tee Launch

It is an inter department mega event of ILLUMINATI, where all the departments have to design their own T-shirt that symbolises their respective department. They launch their t-shirt in front of the entire college via a fashion show and a ramp walk and every year the students are contributing to this event to make it more graceful. Every department’s t-shirt is unique and they try all the best possible ways to present it in front of the audience.
