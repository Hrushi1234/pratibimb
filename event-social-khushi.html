Khushi

Khushi: The glimpses of our 700 kgs of Khushi , wherein we went door to door in the surrounding locality collecting raddi and clothes from homes. All the donation was given to Helpage India. We visited their old age home, spent time with the old yet young-at-heart ladies there. The many boxes of clothes went to the needy and the toys went to all cute little kids. This drive was recognized by Maharashtra Times, Saamna, Navakal.
