Station Wall Painting

The cultural group of Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute on 29th September 2019 painted the walls of Matunga Station, the main intention behind this wall painting was to create awareness among the masses about importance of climate, mother earth and why we should maintain a balance in nature. The students enthusiastically took participate in this wall painting. The paintings beautifully depict the elements of nature and delivers a strong message to the audience. “Don’t be cruel, Conserve your fuel” and “Don’t be Greedy, it’s time to be Greeny”, such impeccable quotes are written on the walls of station. These quotes not only stress on nature conservation but also creates a sense of responsibility among the citizens. So more and more colleges should undertake such painting drives for creating awareness about our precious nature and conservation of mother earth.
