About Us:

Pratibimb, Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute's Socio-cultural extravaganza is a national level celebration enveloping Mumbai's young and artistic minds into an amalgamation of creativity and imagination. Entering it's 32nd year, Pratibimb VJTI has now established itself as a brand with a proud legacy and an unflinching zeal of achieving it's mission - Help one find his reflection. Termed as the Mirror of Emotions, Pratibimb VJTI hopes to raise the bar in year 2019, with double the entertainment and double the fun! Come aboard and hop on to one of the most beautiful journeys of seeking the soul of your artistic self.

Events:

Mega Events

Mega Events is the most prestigious events of Pratibimb. It includes Aarambh's Freshers Party, Garbha Night, Music Concerts, EDM Nights, Pratibimb Synergy and many other events

Illuminati

Illuminati is the inter departmental cultural battle between the 11 departments in VJTI to achieve the Illuminati Cup which is awarded to the department with most points by the end of Pratibimb.

Peforming Arts

VJTI brings to you PRATIBIMB's inter-college event Performing Arts which will be an amalgamation of some of the most classic competitions.

Social Drive

Social Drive is an a step by Pratibimb towards spreading social awarness
